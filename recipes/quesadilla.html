Quesadilla

Description

An Air Fryer Quesadilla is one of my favorite quick, easy lunch recipes. It’s done in 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas: Large ones are easiest to fold and hold more filling without falling apart. Use soft corn tortillas for a gluten-free option.
  • Shredded cheese: The best cheese for quesadillas is a good melty cheese, like Monterey Jack, cheddar, or a Mexican blend.
  • Butter: Gives the tortilla a golden, crisp finish. You could use cooking spray but it’s less flavorful.

Optional Fillings

You can keep it as a simple cheese quesadilla, or add some fillings for extra protein, nutrients and flavor.

  • Proteins: Shredded chicken (like rotisserie chicken), ground beef, black beans, refried beans, bacon.
  • Vegetables: Sauteed spinach, peppers, mushrooms, etc.
  • Repurposed leftovers: Like leftover taco meat, shredded beef, steak fajita filling, chicken street taco filling, tacos Al Pastor pork, leftover turkey etc.
  • Pizza version: You could even use jarred pizza sauce, pepperoni and mozzarella for a yummy pizza version.

Dips and toppings:

  • Creamy toppings: Like Greek yogurt, Sour cream, baja sauce, cilantro lime crema and/or guacamole
  • Spicy toppings: Like taco salsa, hot sauce (like mango habanero sauce!), etc.
  • Fresh toppings: Fresh cilantro, pickled red onion, sliced jalapenos, a squeeze of lime juice, etc.

Steps

  1. Prep the Tortilla: Work on a flat surface. Spread butter on one side of each tortilla. This helps it crisp up like a grilled cheese.
  2. Add the Filling: Lay the tortilla butter-side down. Sprinkle cheese evenly on one half. Add any extra fillings on top of the cheese. Fold the tortilla in half to make a half-moon shape.
  3. Air Fry Until Crispy: Preheat the air fryer to 375°F (190°C). Place the folded quesadilla in the air-fryer basket in a single layer. Cook 3 minutes, flip carefully, then cook 2 to 3 minutes more until golden brown and melty inside.
  4. Slice and Serve: Let the quesadilla cool for a minute so the cheese sets slightly. Cut into wedges with a pizza cutter, sharp knife, or scissors. Serve warm with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole.
